ITC Building

Building Information

Year of Construction: 2003

No. of Storey: 5-storey

Building Type: Office and School

View Infrastructure Audit

The Information Technology Center (ITC) Building serves as both an administrative hub and an academic facility dedicated to Information Technology students. It houses various offices, computer laboratories, and lecture rooms designed to support digital learning and technical training.

Recommendation

It is highly recommended for the building to install fire safety equipment such as sprinkler, fire hose cabinet, and smoke detector. In addition to that, an evacuation plan is also necessary considering that it is a high-storey with a lot of rooms inside.
