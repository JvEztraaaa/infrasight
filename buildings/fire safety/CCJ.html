College of Criminal Justice (CCJ)

Building Information

Year of Construction: 2018

No. of Storey: 4-storey

Building Type: Office and School

View Infrastructure Audit

The College of Criminal Justice, established in 2018, is a modern academic facility that functions as both an office and instructional building dedicated to the field of law enforcement and criminology. It houses faculty offices, classrooms, and specialized training areas designed to support a comprehensive curriculum for criminal justice students.
