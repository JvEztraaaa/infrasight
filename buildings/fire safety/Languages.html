Languages and Humanities Building (CAS)

Building Information

Year of Construction: 2002

No. of Storey: 3-storey

Building Type: Office and School

View Infrastructure Audit

The Languages and Humanities Building, commonly referred to as the College of Arts and Sciences (CAS), is a key academic facility that fosters interdisciplinary learning in the fields of language, humanities, and the sciences. It encompasses both the Physical Sciences and Biological Sciences Buildings, which provide essential spaces for classroom instruction, laboratory experimentation, and scholarly engagement.
