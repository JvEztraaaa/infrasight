Engineering Building (DIET)

Building Information

Year of Construction: 1998

No. of Storey: 3-storey

The Engineering Building (3-storey), is an office and school building used by the Department of Industrial Engineering and Technology (DIET) students and faculty. It was constructed in the year 1998, designed to serve as a learning and office space.
