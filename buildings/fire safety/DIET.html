Engineering Building (DIET)

Building Information

Year of Construction: 1998

No. of Storey: 3-storey

Building Type: Office and School

View Infrastructure Audit

The Engineering Building (3-storey) is an office and school building used by the Department of Industrial Engineering and Technology (DIET) students and faculty. It was constructed in the year 2006, designed to serve as a learning and office space.

Recommendation

It is highly recommended that the building install sprinkler systems along the building. Although the Engineering Building (DIET) appeared to be almost compliant, it is suggested to update the evacuation plan as it lacks the complete information needed. See documentation photos for reference.
