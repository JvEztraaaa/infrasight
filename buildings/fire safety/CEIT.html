Building Information

Year of Construction: 2006

No. of Storey: 2-storey

Building Type: Office

View Infrastructure Audit

The College of Engineering Information and Technology, or CEIT, was constructed in 2006. A two-storey building used as an office for faculties of Department of Engineering and Architecture (DCEA), Department of Computer, Electronics, and Electrical Engineering (DCEEE), Department of Industrial Engineering and Technology (DIET), Department of Information Technology (DIT), Department of Agriculture and Food Engineering (DAFE), and Physical Planning Unit (PPU).

Recommendation

It is strongly recommended that the College of Engineering and Information Technology (CEIT) prioritize the installation of essential fire protection equipment. The absence of sprinklers, fire hose cabinets, and smoke detectors poses a significant risk to the safety of building occupants during fire emergencies. It is imperative that the administration allocate resources for the immediate procurement and installation of these systems to ensure early fire detection, effective suppression, and access to firefighting tools.
