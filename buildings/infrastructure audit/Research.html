Interdisciplinary Research Building

Building Information

Year of Construction: 2016

No. of Storey: 17-storey

Building Type: Industrial

View Fire Safety Inspection

The Interdisciplinary Research Building, constructed in 2016, is a modern industrial-type facility designed to support innovative research across various academic and scientific fields. As a hub for collaborative investigations and advanced experimentation, it houses specialized laboratories, research equipment, and workspaces for faculty and students engaged in interdisciplinary studies.

Accessibility Features:
- Elevator with Braille System
- PWD CR with Grab Bar

Recommendation

While only minor structural defects were officially recorded, multiple cracks on various structural elements, including both exterior and interior structural elements, and notably on exterior elevator walls across several floor levels, raised significant concerns about the building's overall seismic performance. Given these conditions, special attention should be given to the cracks in critical structural elements and elevator walls, which may indicate underlying stress or material fatigue that could worsen during seismic activity. Regular inspections and maintenance of auxiliary systems should also be implemented to ensure the long-term safety and functionality of the facility.
